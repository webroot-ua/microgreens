MICROGREENS PEAS

  1. Richness in Vitamins and Minerals:

    Microgreens of peas contain a significant amount of vitamin C (66.7%), moderate amounts of vitamins B1 thiamine (12.5%) and B5 pantothenic acid (15.0%). They are particularly rich in vitamin B9 folate (10.5%), a nutrient essential for DNA synthesis and cell division. They are rich in the minerals iron (11.6%) and manganese (10.6%), as well as choline (10.5%), which is essential for many processes in the body, maintaining heart health, strengthening the immune system and detoxification.

  2. High Level of Antioxidants:

    Antioxidants such as carotenoids and flavonoids protect cells from free radical damage.

  3. Improving the Gastrointestinal Support System:

    Fiber content helps to normalize digestion and intestinal function.

  4. Maintaining Skin Health:

    Vitamin C stimulates collagen synthesis, which ensures healthy and elastic skin.

  5. Low Calorie Content:

    Ideal for those who are watching their weight due to its low calorie and high nutrient content.

  6. Increasing Iron Levels:

    Iron content helps to maintain normal hemoglobin levels in the blood, which is important for the prevention of anemia.

  7. Reducing the Risk of Heart Disease:

    Phytonutrients such as isoflavones help reduce the risk of heart disease and regulate cholesterol levels.

  8. Nutritional Value for the Eyes:

    Lutein and zeaxanthin promote eye health and prevent retinal degeneration.
